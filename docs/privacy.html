Armenian Keyboard+ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

In short: Armenian Keyboard+ operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Armenian Keyboard+ does not collect any data. Specifically, the app does not:

  • Collect or record anything you type
  • Gather usage statistics or device information
  • Use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks
  • Request or require Full Access permissions
  • Connect to any third-party services or APIs
  • Make any network requests whatsoever

Word Predictions

Word suggestions and next-word predictions are powered by an on-device language model. All text processing happens locally on your device. No text is ever sent to external servers.

Permissions

The keyboard extension operates using only standard keyboard permissions provided by iOS. The app does not request Full Access, which means iOS enforces that the keyboard cannot access the network or communicate with other apps.

Data Storage

The app does not store any personal data. The only data persisted on your device is the keyboard's built-in language model, which ships with the app and contains no user data.

Children's Privacy

Armenian Keyboard+ does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
